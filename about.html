About 22 AVA

22 AVA is a premier artist management company dedicated to shaping iconic careers through strategic vision, creative development, and global industry connections.

Who We Are

22 AVA (Artist Vision & Advancement) represents forward-thinking music talent and emerging artists across genres. Our team focuses on long-term career development, brand positioning, and creating meaningful opportunities within the modern music industry.

Our Mission

At 22 AVA, our mission is to develop iconic artists through strategic management, creative vision, and sustainable career building. We believe in cultivating authentic talent and empowering artists to reach their full potential in an evolving global music landscape.

What Sets Us Apart

Artist-First Strategy

We prioritize the creative identity and long-term vision of each artist, ensuring every decision aligns with their brand, values, and career goals.

Industry Network

22 AVA collaborates with labels, promoters, brands, and industry professionals to create meaningful opportunities and strategic partnerships for artists.

Modern Career Development

We integrate traditional management with digital strategy, audience growth, and brand development to build scalable, future-ready careers.

Our Approach

22 AVA takes a holistic approach to artist management, combining creative direction, business strategy, and cultural positioning. By aligning artistic vision with industry insight, we help artists navigate the complexities of the music industry and achieve lasting impact.

Areas of Expertise

Career Strategy & Representation

Long-term planning, professional representation, contract negotiation, and strategic career guidance.

Brand Development & Creative Direction

Visual identity, storytelling, audience engagement, and positioning within the music industry.

Touring & Partnerships

Tour strategy, brand collaborations, industry partnerships, and global opportunity development.

Our Vision

Our vision is to shape the next generation of artists by combining creative innovation with strategic management. 22 AVA aims to become a leading force in artist development and cultural influence within the global music industry.
